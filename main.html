Hyderabad


A vibrant city with the imposing Charminar at its heart, Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a potpourri of history, cuisine and eclectic experiences. While its Old City area is an impressive heritage haven with iconic monuments dotting its narrow lanes, its contemporary counterpart matches pace with its cosmopolitan population.

  1. Ramoji Film City
  2. Golconda Fort
  3. Birla Mandir
  4. Salar Jung Museum
  5. Sri Jagannath Temple

  • Phirni
  • Malai Paya
